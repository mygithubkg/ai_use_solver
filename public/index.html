Frequently Asked Questions

What is AI Summary Pro?

AI Summary Pro is an advanced tool that uses artificial intelligence to transform complaints, causes, and corrections into professional, comprehensive summaries. It's perfect for business reports, documentation, and professional communication.

How does the summary generation work?

You provide three key pieces of information: the complaint, the root cause, and the proposed correction. Our AI then analyzes this information and generates a professional summary with five detailed sections covering all aspects of your case.

What format does the summary come in?

Each summary includes five sections: Summary (overview), More about the Complaint, What Might Have Caused It, Planned Correction, and Why It Is Important to Fix It. Each section is approximately 100 words and written in professional language.

Is my data secure?

Yes, your data is processed securely and is not permanently stored. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information, and all processing is done through secure API connections.

Can I use this for business purposes?

Absolutely! AI Summary Pro is designed specifically for business use. The summaries are professional, well-structured, and perfect for reports, documentation, and communication with stakeholders.

How can I maximize the benefits of this tool?

To get the best results, provide detailed and specific information in each field. Be clear about the complaint, thorough in identifying the root cause, and comprehensive in describing your solution. The more detailed your input, the better your summary will be.
